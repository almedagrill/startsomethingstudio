Start Something Studio

I help creative entrepreneurs turn ideas into real things that build community.

I’m Francis Almeda, a builder of brands, spaces, and community.

For the past five years, I’ve helped launch some of Chicago’s most loved cafes, a novel pizzeria, a culture-driven bodega, while helping other entrepreneurs bring their visions to life. Before hospitality, I spent 20+ years in advertising crafting creative work for brands big and small.

Now, I’m bringing it all together to create meaningful experiences people can gather around.

What I Do

Idea to reality, end to end.

Whether it’s a restaurant, a retail concept, a brand launch, or a side project that’s ready to become a real thing, I help turn “what if” into “we’re open.” As a creative partner, operator, or advisor for teams already in motion.

I
Concept & Strategy
Naming, positioning, audience strategy, experience design, and go-to-market plan.
II
Brand & Creative Direction
Visual identity guidance, packaging, content systems, and creative direction.
III
Launch & Operations
Build coordination, vendor sourcing, workflows, staffing, and the opening playbook.
IV
Marketing & Momentum
Launch campaigns, partnerships, influencer seeding, community building, and content cadence.

Available for: brands, agencies, founders, aspiring entrepreneurs, and investment groups.
